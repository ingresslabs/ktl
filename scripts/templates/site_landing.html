Build, verify, plan, apply

Agent-first Kubernetes delivery CLI.

torque is a file-first loop for Kubernetes delivery: build, verify, plan, apply, capture evidence, and inspect what happened. Captures, verifier reports, and chart archives are portable SQLite artifacts for CI, review, and later debugging without a running service.
